Support

How are winnings paid out?

Daily resolution happens at 23:59 UTC. The smart contract splits 80% of the daily pool among the top 3 scorers proportionally based on their score.

What tokens can I use?

We support USDm, USDT, and USDC on Celo Mainnet.

Can I enter more than once per day?

No, one entry per wallet per day for the Daily Challenge.

Where can I see the smart contract?

You can view the transparent logic on Celoscan.

I had a transaction issue — what do I do?

Email us at: support@vaultrun.xyz
